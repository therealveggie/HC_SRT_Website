Project History

import_contacts

Signing of the Product Charter

September 30, 2016

The second version of the SRT project charter was signed. This marked the beginning of the project.

flag

Phase I: Autopopulation Tool Rollout

October 15, 2016

The autopopulate add-in was finalized and released to the general users. The second phase (Screening tool) was in its beta-phase and released to a handful of beta-testers. The documentation access tool was also in its beta-phase and released to beta-testers.

flag

Phase II: Screening Tool Rollout

January 15, 2017

The screening tool was finalized and released to the general users. The third phase (Review tool) for document access had also completed beta testing and released to a handful of users. The issues management tool was well under way in development while the business reporting tool was being brainstormed.

flag

Phase III: Full Suite Rollout

March 30, 2017

The issue management and business reporting tools have been completed and beta-tested. The full suite of tools is now available for general release and will continually be updated for better usability.
